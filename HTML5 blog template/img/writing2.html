Will we really find any signs of life beyond Earth?

Astrobiology is a science with little to no evidence. We know there is life here on Earth and, because Earth is in no way separate to the rest of the universe, we know that there is life in the universe. However, when it comes to looking for life beyond Earth we really have nothing much to go on...yet. By studying life on Earth we know increasingly more about where life could live and how it might get started...
